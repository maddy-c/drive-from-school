Summary

The Lego Movie is about Emmet, a completely normal person, who ends up being the chosen one in a prophecy to save the universe from the evil Lord Business, who wants to glue everything together. It follows him and the friends he makes along the way as they quest to stop Lord Business before doomsday. I also really like Benny the Spaceman from the movie, he's my favorite character.
